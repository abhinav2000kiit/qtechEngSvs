Our Supplies

Ash Conditioner

The ash conditioner is used to condition Ashes and other abrasive materials. It mixes properly the materials and water with the help of paddle type shafts in a closed body preceding to discharge.

Denseveyor

The Denseveyor is a dense phase pneumatic conveying carrier. It provides a dust free conveying system which stops spreading the dust into the environment. It can work automatically on an as required basis and can be used for conveying various powder form materials and compressed gases.

Ash Vessel

It is a cast Iron vessel and controls the flow of Ash with the pneumatically operated dome valve and transfers the ash through standard pipelines at low velocity. The opening and closing timing of Dome valve is set with the help of pneumatic panels.
